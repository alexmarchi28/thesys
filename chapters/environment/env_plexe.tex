\section{Plexe: Platooning Simulation and Scenarios}
\label{env:plexe}

PLEXE, \emph{Platooning Extension for Veins}, extends Veins to explicitly support cooperative platooning simulation by introducing components dedicated to longitudinal control, vehicle dynamics, intra-platoon communication protocols, and the management of scenarios and maneuvers \cite{segata2014plexe}. For the present work, PLEXE represents the software layer in which the link between communication and control takes concrete form: it is the framework that makes it possible to model platoon behavior beyond SUMO road mobility alone.

PLEXE provides longitudinal controllers, namely ACC and CACC, vehicle dynamics models to represent responses to acceleration and braking commands, tools for platoon management and for assigning vehicle roles within simulation scenarios, and integration with the networking layer through Veins and OMNeT++, thus acting as a specialized extension for the platooning domain.
