\section{Thesis Objectives}

In light of this context, the thesis pursues four main objectives:
\begin{enumerate}
\item define an updated and reproducible experimental environment for simulating multi-technology V2X scenarios, integrating OMNeT++, Veins, PLEXE, SUMO, and Simu5G;
\item recover from the reference work its main scenarios, metrics, and logic, with particular attention to PDR estimation and the \emph{SafeSwitch} mechanism;
\item replace the cellular component based on SimuLTE with a 5G model built on Simu5G, adapting the \texttt{plexe\_hetnet} module and its related interfaces accordingly;
\item design an experimental campaign to verify the functional consistency of the integration and evaluate possible differences in the results with respect to the previous work.
\end{enumerate}

The goal is not to propose a new control algorithm, but rather to critically reproduce, update, and validate a significant experimental setup from recent literature. In this sense, the value of the work lies both in the correct reconstruction of the reference system and in its extension toward a more up-to-date cellular component and toolchain.
