\chapter{SafeSwitch and 5G}
\label{ch:safeswitch_5g}

The chapter opens with a description of the multi-technology architecture in Section~\ref{sec:s5g_architecture}. On this basis, the first part presents \emph{SafeSwitch} at the functional level, with attention to PDR monitoring in Section~\ref{sec:s5g_monitoring}, the state machine in Section~\ref{sec:s5g_fsm}, and the fallback, recovery, and gap adaptation mechanism in Section~\ref{sec:s5g_gap}. The second part instead deals with the migration to the 5G cellular component, discussed in Section~\ref{sec:s5g_5g}, while keeping the role of the cellular network unchanged within the multi-technology logic.
