\chapter{Preparation of the Experimental Environment}
\label{ch:setup}

This appendix documents the operational construction of the environment used for the simulations. The goal is to make explicit the procedure through which a toolchain compatible with the multi-technology study was assembled, while remaining consistent with the official instructions of Plexe and its related subprojects.

The description is intentionally technical: it includes workspace organization, compilation sequence, structure of the simulation files, namely NED, INI, and SUMO, checks for correct operation, and automation of the run campaigns. Where necessary, variants adopted in the thesis work with respect to standard procedures are reported, especially for SUMO and OMNeT++.
